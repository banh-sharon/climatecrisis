BLACKPINK is a globally renowned South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Known for their powerful music, striking visuals, and innovative performances, BLACKPINK has become one of the most influential K-pop acts worldwide. Beyond their musical impact, the members also use their platform to support various social causes, including climate change. They have partnered with organizations like Climate Action Now and raised awareness on environmental issues. Through their influential presence in fashion and media, BLACKPINK encourages sustainability, promoting eco-friendly fashion choices and advocating for a more conscious approach to consumption. They represent a blend of talent and social responsibility, using their voice to inspire positive change for the environment.

Jisoo(Kim Ji-soo)

  • Birthdate: January 3, 1995
  • Position: Lead vocalist, visual
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • Known for: Strong vocal skills, stunning visual
  • Fun fact: She is the oldest member of BLACKPINK
  • Jisoo is focusing on her solo music career. Her debut solo single album, ME, was released in March 2023.

Jennie(Kim Jennie)

  • Birthdate: January 16, 1996
  • Position: Main rapper, lead vocalist
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • Known for: Versatile rap and vocal skills, "solo" debut
  • Fun fact: She is often called "Human Chanel" for her high fashion sense.
  • Jennie continues to balance BLACKPINK's activities with her solo career. She also pursues fashion, acting, and various endorsements, and made her acting debut in the series The Idol in 2023.

Rosé(Park Chae-young)

  • Birthdate: February 11, 1997
  • Position: Main vocalist, lead dancer
  • Nationality: New Zealand (born), South Korean
  • Known for: Unique voice, iconic guitar performance in On The Ground
  • Fun fact: She is left-handed and has a love for playing the guitar.
  • Rosé is working on her solo career, having released her debut single album R in 2021. She is also involved in brand endorsements and is active in fashion.

Lisa (Lalisa Manoban)

  • Birthdate: March 27, 1997
  • Position: Main dancer, lead rapper, sub-vocalist
  • Nationality: Thai
  • Known for: Exceptional dancing, being the first non-Korean BLACKPINK member
  • Fun fact: Lisa is fluent in multiple languages, including Thai, Korean, and English.
  • Lisa released her solo album LALISA in 2021 and has been focusing on solo music and dance projects. She is also active in fashion, collaborating with major luxury brands.
